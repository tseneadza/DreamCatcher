# DreamCatcher Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Your Journal Is Private

DreamCatcher is a personal dream journal. Everything you write -- dream entries, sleep logs, goals, ideas, and AI interpretations -- is stored privately and belongs to you. We do not read, sell, or share your personal journal content with anyone.

## What We Store for You

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Email & name** | Account login and display |
| **Dream entries** | Title, content, mood, tags, date, AI interpretation |
| **Sleep logs** | Sleep/wake times, quality rating, notes |
| **Goals** | Title, description, category, status, milestones |
| **Ideas** | Content, category, tags, priority |

All of this data is tied to your account and is never visible to other users.

## Optional Research Participation

You may choose to contribute anonymized dream data to sleep and dream research. **This is entirely optional.** You will never be asked to participate as a condition of using the app.

If you opt in, the following **de-identified** data points are extracted from your entries and stored separately from your account:

- **Emotion labels** (e.g., "joy," "fear") -- not your written words
- **Theme categories** (e.g., "flying," "water") -- not your dream content
- **Lucid dream flag** (yes/no)
- **Time band** (e.g., weekday vs. weekend) -- not your exact sleep times
- **Mood score** (1-5)
- **Sleep quality score** (1-5)
- **Age bracket and region** (if voluntarily provided) -- never your name or email

Your identity (user ID, email, name) is **never** stored in research tables. Research records cannot be traced back to you.

## How to Opt Out

You can revoke your research consent at any time from **Settings > Research Participation**. When you revoke:

- Your consent record is marked as revoked with a timestamp
- **No new data** is extracted from your entries going forward
- Previously contributed research events are **permanently deleted**
- Aggregate statistics (counts and averages already computed) are retained because they contain no individual-level data

## Data Retention

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Personal journal data | Kept as long as your account is active. Deleted when you delete your account. |
| Research events | Deleted upon consent revocation or account deletion. |
| Aggregate research data | Retained indefinitely (contains no personal data). |
| Account credentials | Deleted immediately upon account deletion. |

## Third-Party Sharing

- **Personal data:** Never shared with third parties.
- **Research data:** Only aggregate, de-identified statistics may be shared with academic research partners. No individual-level data is ever shared.
- **AI processing:** Dream interpretations are generated via OpenAI. Only the content you explicitly request interpretation for is sent. We do not send your identity or metadata.
